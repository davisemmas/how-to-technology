Introduction to AI Need help? Ask Chatty Cathy →

Artificial Intelligence, often called AI, is technology that helps computers do tasks that usually require human thinking. AI is when a computer is programmed to recognize patterns, make suggestions, answer questions, and learn from information. You may already be using AI without realizing it. In this lesson, you will learn what AI is and how it shows up in everyday life.

Where Might I See AI?
Companies have been increasing the amount of AI tools available in their products over the last couple of years. You may encounter AI when your phone suggests the next word while typing, a website recommends movies or products, you use voice assistants like Siri or Alexa, or email filters remove spam messages.

★ Important Tip ★
AI does not think or feel like a person would. It follows instructions created by people. AI is a tool. It should help you, not replace your judgment or make important decisions for you.
